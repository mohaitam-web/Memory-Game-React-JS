                          Features

1- Finish the game after time

2- Every right choose adds 5 score for the player and
add the card to the player

3- The first card is shown for 5 seconds if the second 
card isn't chosen yet.

4- The player creates its name and save it in the local storage

5- After every game show the winner banner

6- Save the game details in the local storage

7- Once any player has won 7 times show the final winner
and reset the game
